XYLEM LEARNING

XYLEM is a popular educational platform based in Kerala. It provides coaching for school students as well as entrance exams like NEET, JEE, and KEAM. The institution offers both online and offline classes for better learning. It was started in 2020 with the aim of improving education for students. Xylem Learning provides live classes, recorded videos, and study materials. It also has many centers across South India to support students. The platform helps students achieve their academic goals through proper guidance. Today, it is known as one of the fast-growing learning platforms in Kerala
